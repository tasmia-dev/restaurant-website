The Beginning

A Dream Born Over a Family Dinner

The Golden Fork was founded in 2008 by Chef Arjun Mehra and his wife Priya, inspired by a simple dream — to create a space where food could bring people together, create memories, and celebrate the extraordinary flavours of India and the world.

What started as a 30-seat bistro on Church Street has grown into one of Bengaluru's most celebrated dining destinations, while never losing the warmth and intimacy of that first table.

Today, we serve over 300 guests nightly, but every plate is still crafted with the same attention and care as that very first dinner service.

Our Philosophy

Food as an Art Form

We believe that a great meal is a conversation — between land and sea, between tradition and innovation, between the chef and the guest. Every dish we create is a reflection of our respect for ingredients, our love for technique, and our commitment to giving you an experience that lasts long after the last bite.

Locally Sourced

We partner directly with farms across Karnataka and the Western Ghats for the freshest produce available.

Artisan Craft

From house-cured meats to hand-rolled pasta, we do things the slow, careful, traditional way.

Seasonal Menus

Our menu changes with the seasons — celebrating what nature provides at its very best each month.

The Chef

Chef Arjun Mehra

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Arjun trained at the Culinary Institute of America before spending a decade in Paris under the legendary Chef Pierre Dupont. His return to India in 2008 brought with him a unique fusion of classical French technique with a deep love for Indian flavours and ingredients.

Under his leadership, The Golden Fork has received numerous accolades and has been consistently ranked among South India's top 10 restaurants. But for Chef Arjun, the greatest reward remains the smiles of guests at his tables.

"I don't cook to impress. I cook to connect. Every plate is a handshake between me and the person sitting at that table."
