Our Story

A Passion for Food,
A Love for Hospitality

Born in the heart of Bengaluru, The Golden Fork has been a destination for those who seek more than just a meal — they seek an experience.

Our culinary philosophy is rooted in celebrating the finest local ingredients, paired with global techniques and a deep respect for traditional recipes. Every dish is a conversation between the past and the present.
